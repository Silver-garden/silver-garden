Our Journey

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Malout, Punjab, Silver Garden Cafeteria began as a small coffee stand with a big dream - to bring exceptional coffee experiences to our community.

What started as a humble kiosk has blossomed into a beloved local destination where coffee lovers gather to enjoy carefully sourced beans, expertly prepared beverages, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Every cup we serve reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the art of coffee making. We take pride in supporting local farmers and suppliers while delivering an authentic cafe experience.
