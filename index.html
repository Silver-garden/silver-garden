Our Story

Founded in 2025, Silver Garden Cafeteria began as a small coffee stand with a big dream - to bring exceptional coffee experiences to our community in Malout, Punjab.

Today, we've grown into a beloved local destination where coffee lovers gather to enjoy carefully sourced beans, expertly prepared beverages, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Every cup we serve reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the art of coffee making.
